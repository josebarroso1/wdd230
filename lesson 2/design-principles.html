Visual Hierarchy


USAXE CLUB


usaxeclub.com

The website draws attention to the club's main attraction, which is the axe-throwing, then it gives a small explanation about the main attraction and then it gives a great focus on the most important thing, which is the reservation to carry out the axe-throwing.

Proximity


Gucci


Gucci.com

Undoubtedly the site gives us the message that it is a website for selling articles such as clothes and other accessories, the very image used draws attention to the clothes and the accessory, the phrase in evidence appeals to us to see the articles of good appearance and the following item in a box relates to the sentence because it leads us to see more and buy.

Contrast


Creative Inspiration


creativeinspiration.com

The site shows us a contrast of appealing colors that match the site's name and logo, the high quality images show the site's theme and its expertise, the letters and the color of the same are legible, simple and beautiful and the great contrast that draws attention is the small bar at the bottom that asks us to get in touch.
